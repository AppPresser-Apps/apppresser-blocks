Effortlessly add animated blob backgrounds to your posts and pages.

== Description ==

Blobs is a dynamic addition to your WordPress site, providing a visually captivating block for the Block editor. This plugin seamlessly integrates into the block editor, allowing you to effortlessly add animated blob backgrounds to your posts and pages. Enhance the visual appeal of your site with Animated Blobs and captivate your audience with engaging visuals.

1. Customizable Blobs: Choose from a variety of pre-designed blob shapes or create your own custom blobs to match your site's aesthetic.
2. Animation Effects: Add life to your blobs with smooth animation effects. Select from options like gentle pulsation, subtle movement, or dynamic swirling to enhance the visual appeal of your content.
3. Color Options: Customize the color scheme of your blobs to perfectly complement your site's branding or the overall design of your content.
4. Opacity Control: Adjust the opacity of the blobs to create layered effects or subtle overlays, allowing your content to shine through while still maintaining the visual interest provided by the animated blobs.
5. Responsive Design: The Animated Blob Background Block is fully responsive, ensuring that your blobs look stunning on all devices, from desktops to smartphones.
6. Easy Integration: Simply add the block to your post or page using the Gutenberg editor, and effortlessly customize the settings to achieve the desired look and feel.
7. Lightweight and Fast: Built with performance in mind, this plugin is lightweight and optimized for speed, ensuring that your site remains fast and responsive even with animated blob backgrounds.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/blobs` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Open the block editor and add a new block.
4. Search for "Animated Blob Background" block and add it to your content.
5. Customize the blob shapes, colors, animation effects, and opacity settings to match your preferences.
6. Preview your changes and publish your post or page to see the animated blob background in action!
